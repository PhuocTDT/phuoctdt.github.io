### Week 6 Objectives:

- Master Identity and Access Management (IAM)
- Deep dive into IAM Policies (JSON, Condition keys)
- Understand IAM Roles for EC2 and Trust Relationships
- Configure IAM Identity Center (SSO) and Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
- Learn and implement Resource-based Policies

### Week 6 Outcomes:

- Mastered **IAM security models**, ensuring only authorized users and services can access cloud resources.
- Gained a deep understanding of **Advanced IAM Policies**, enabling precise control over cloud infrastructure.
- Successfully implemented **IAM Roles**, eliminating the risk of hardcoded credentials on servers.
- Established a secure login environment using **SSO and MFA**, mitigating the risk of unauthorized access.
- Confidently implemented **Resource-based Policies**, providing an additional layer of security directly on data and keys.
